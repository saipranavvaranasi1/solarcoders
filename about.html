Home/About

Every kid deserves a launchpad into the technology shaping their world.

$>SolarCoders was founded by a middle-school student who believed learning to code shouldn't depend on your zip code, your budget, or knowing someone in tech.

Our mission

Less like a classroom, more like a mission worth showing up for.

We teach Python — the language behind modern AI — in a way that feels like exploration, not homework. No prior experience, no expensive gear. Curiosity, a browser, and a crew that has your back.

Beginner-first Ages 8–14 Live mentors Small crews
  1. 01

    Real code from the first session

    Students write working Python on day one — we skip the lectures and go straight to building.

  2. 02

    AI, understood — not just used

    Kids build chatbots and smart tools while learning how the technology actually reasons.

  3. 03

    No one drifts off course

    A 1:6 mentor ratio means every learner is seen, unstuck quickly, and never left behind.

  4. 04

    Free, and meant to stay that way

    Funded by donors and run by volunteers so cost is never the reason a kid can't start.

Why families choose us

Built for real kids — not just future engineers.

Python from day one

We start with the language professionals actually use — no toy blocks that don't transfer.

Real AI, made clear

Students build with AI and understand how it works — demystified, hands-on, age-appropriate.

Small crews, real attention

A 1:6 mentor ratio means every kid is seen, unstuck quickly, and never left behind.

Safe & supportive

Vetted volunteers, a kind culture, and a no-judgment space where mistakes power learning.

Project-based, always

Every session ends with something built and shareable — proof that learning is happening.

Open to all

No tuition, no gatekeeping. If a kid wants to learn, there's a seat in the rocket.
